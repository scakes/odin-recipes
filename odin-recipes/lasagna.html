Lasagna Recipe

Description:

I can't think of a description for lasagna so please just pretend this is a description for lasagna. Also most of this was stolen from google and this is (sadly) not a legit lasagna recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • your first-born child
  • 16 ounces whole wheat lasagna
  • 3/4 cup chopped mushrooms
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 2 ounce pasta sauce
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 cup shredded parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles in a large pot of boiling water for 10 minutes, or until al dente. Rinse with cold water, and drain
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a hot saucepan, add in the garlic and mushrooms
  3. Let simmer for 10 minute
  4. Mix lasagna with basil, sauce and parmesan cheese
  5. Provide an offering to the devil with your first-born child to obtain a mystery item. Don't worry if no Item appears, that just means your pasta is already perfect and doesn't need any items to touch it up
  6. Incorporate all ingredients and bake in the oven at 350 F for 10 minutes
  7. Enjoy :)
